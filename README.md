## Comprehensive Kidney Disease Management

Kidney diseases often progress silently, making regular health check-ups essential. Dr. Kamal Kiran emphasizes early diagnosis through advanced investigations and personalized treatment strategies.

Patients experiencing the following symptoms should seek medical consultation:

* Swelling in the feet or face
* Reduced urine output
* Persistent high blood pressure
* Blood in urine
* Protein in urine
* Fatigue and weakness
* Frequent urinary infections

Timely intervention can significantly slow disease progression and reduce the need for dialysis or transplantation.
